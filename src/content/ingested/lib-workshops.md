WHAT IT IS:

SMHD is really a "LIVE-IT" - it's about LIVING more peacefully, healthfully and joyfully. Like any good diet we take some things out and we add some things in. Through the wisdom practices we will weed out and release the limiting factors in body and mind, while we build a toolkit that will keep you connected to your natural brightest essence. HUMAN means - "light of the mind" so as we empty the mind of clutter we fill with light. Drawing mainly from Yoga and Ayurveda as well as soulful and healthful practices from other cultures, we will travel week by week through Mind, Body and Spirit. This course is rooted in actionable practical applications to decrease the stress responses in the body and mind which results in a healthier relationship with yourself and others. The program will culminate in creating your personalized daily practice (Sadhana) drawing on all that you have covered and experienced in the previous weeks, with Christine's guidance and the powerful energy of the group!

WHO IS CHRISTINE BURKE...

Christine is a lifelong student and teacher of 23 years of all things related to personal development, health and lifestyle practices, yoga and yoga philosophy, emotional and physical well being. She is passionate about supporting the elevation of your life through empowered choices and active awareness and compassion. Though her first memory of spiritual pondering was at the age of 8 when she became keenly aware that we have only this moment, it wasn't until her 20's that she truly "found yoga". Christine stepped into yoga as an actress & dancer in New York in 1994, which was a life shifting moment for her. In 2000, she received her first certification in Los Angeles as a 200-hour level teacher. She has been teaching full time ever since. She's a Registered Yoga Teacher with Yoga Alliance at 800 hour-plus level and has taught thousands of students of all ages. Christine and her husband, Gary McCleery, opened Liberation Yoga in 2004.. The studio has been named in the 'Top 20 Yoga Studios In The World' by Travel + Leisure Magazine, 'Best Yoga Studio in LA' by LA Weekly and named in the 'Top Ten Most Beautiful Yoga Studios in the U.S. by tripping.com among other awards and accolades. Christine's writing has been featured in LA Yoga and Yogi Times, her teaching featured in LA Times, LA Weekly, Yogi Times, LA Yoga, Westways Magazine, Great Yoga Teachers and many online magazines. Christine's first book, The Yoga Healer, was awarded "The Best Yoga Book" by Soul & Spirit Magazine and her second book "The Power of Hand Yoga, Pranayama and Mudras for Health and Well Being" was also awarded. She has been the yoga & meditation teacher at the renowned Marlborough School since 2012. She is a Grief Educator and workshop facilitator and has been leading her SMHD program 4 times per year since 2018. Christine lives happily and in gratitude for her Liberation community, her family and her LIFE, sharing the gift of yoga in Los Angeles, with her daughter, Clementine, the spirit of her beloved husband, and their companion animals.

What exactly will we be doing?We will be doing a few stretches and using lots of yoga props (pillows, blankets, and blocks) to rest comfortably in gentle poses on the ground.

What is Yoga Nidra?It’s a guided meditation you do while lying on your back. You’ll follow Quinn's voice as we take a rotation of consciousness through your body, breathe deeply, and go on a visualization journey to plant your intention in your mind, body, and psyche. For more information, click here.

What is Reiki energy work?Reiki is a pure energy that flows from the hands of an attuned provider into your body. By hovering our hands over you or gently placing them on your body (with your permission), the energy naturally flows where it’s most needed. Some people report feeling warmth or a pleasant tingling sensation, while others might not feel a thing. Reiki will add to your experience of relaxation, peace, and healing.

The shift can be emotional, physical, psychological, spiritual depending on the flower.

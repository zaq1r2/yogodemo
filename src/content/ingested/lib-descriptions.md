Yin Yoga  |  Yin yoga is a passive practice that focuses on releasing the muscles and getting into our connective tissues. Practice is mostly seated or lying down and poses are held for about five minutes each. We make use of props to create a supported structure for our muscles to relax and let go, allowing gravity to take our bodies down toward the earth.

Physically, yin yoga is appropriate stress for our connective tissues, opens us up to our full range of motion, and brings us towards greater flexibility. More subtly, yin is a gateway to meditation, practice relaxing and calming, and a chance to settle into stillness and quiet.

During this class, we will start with a series of yin asanas and then shift into yoga nidra, a guided journey meditation practiced while lying comfortably on your back. You will leave feeling open and relaxed.

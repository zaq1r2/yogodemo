## The Liberation Yoga Story

Liberation Yoga is a Hatha-based yoga studio in mid-city Los Angeles founded in 2004 by Christine Burke and Gary McCleery. They wanted to build a community and place to make peace with your body, mind, and the world — to find Liberation. They sought an alternative to the corporate yoga vibe and mentality that was fast uprooting independent studios in Los Angeles and beyond.

Liberation Yoga is about living authentically. It's about finding one's true nature through the practice of yoga and sharing this limitless life force with the world. At Liberation we celebrate all styles of Hatha Yoga and all levels of practitioners, recognizing that every individual has a unique path to follow and every teacher has a unique gift to offer.

## What Made It Special

The physical beauty of the space that is Liberation Yoga embodies both the internal and external experience of yoga. The asana room was warm and bright, filled with natural light and an indoor garden resting upon rich, eco-conscious wood floors. Walking into Liberation was like walking into a spacious living room with twinkling star lights that provide a safe, magical environment in which to explore the vast regions of the interior mind, body, and spirit.

- **Award-Winning Studio**: Named one of the "Top 20 Yoga Studios In The World" by Travel and Leisure, "Most Beautiful Yoga Studio" by Hoodline.com and Tripping.com, and "Best Yoga Studio In Los Angeles" by LA Weekly (twice)
- **Inclusive Environment**: Classes for all levels, ages, and body types — from vigorous vinyasa to gentle restorative
- **Community Sanctuary**: A gathering place where friendships formed, support networks grew, and celebrations happened
- **Teacher Development**: Home to a thriving 200-hour teacher training program that launched hundreds of teaching careers
- **Cultural Impact**: A featured location for notable films and television series including "Big Little Lies"

## A Note About the Studio

While the physical studio closed its doors in October 2021 after nearly two decades of service, the spirit of Liberation Yoga lives on. Christine continues to offer classes via Zoom, pop-up workshops in nature locations, retreats, and teacher training under the Liberation Yoga banner. The light that was generated in the studio space continues to fuel the drive, love, and passion to share yoga with the community.
